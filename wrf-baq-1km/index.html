WRF BAQ 1km Forecast

The BAQ 1km Forecast system is running in real time on the HPC cluster at the Universidad del Norte.

Fetches data from NOAA (GFS) and OGIMET (METAR). Surface level observations are interpolated into the GRIB files.

  • Cycle: 00 UTC.
  • Forecast: 03h.
  • GFS: 0.25 degree resolution, global longitude-latitude grid.
  • Isobaric level: 1000 hPa
